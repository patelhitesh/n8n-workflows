Playful Numbered Cards

Hover or focus a card to wake up the doodles ✨

1

Idea

Capture the spark. Quick notes, messy sketches—everything welcome.

2

Define

Frame the problem and success signal—keep it sharp.

3

Research

Talk to humans. Scan patterns. Steal like a designer (ethically).

4

Sketch

Low-fi, high volume. Explore wildly before picking favorites.

5

Prototype

Make it real enough to break. Clicks > decks.

6

Test

Observe, don’t lead. Measure behavior, not vibes.

7

Iterate

Tight loops > huge leaps. Ship small, learn fast.

8

Polish

Trim friction. Add delight. Respect the rhythm.

9

Hand-off

Specs that read like a story. Zero guesswork.

10

Launch

Announce with heart. Celebrate the team. Monitor closely.

11

Measure

KPIs with teeth. Qual + quant in harmony.

12

Grow

Keep compounding. Design the next loop.

Tip: Use Tab to focus cards. Doodles calm down if you prefer reduced motion.
